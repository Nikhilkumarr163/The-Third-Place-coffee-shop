Our Story

More Than Just
Coffee

The Third Place was born in 2018 from a simple belief: every cup of coffee should be a small act of craft. We source beans directly from farms in Coorg, Chikmagalur, and beyond — building relationships that go beyond commerce.

Our baristas train for months before they touch the espresso machine. Our food is made from scratch each morning. Our space is designed to feel like your best friend's living room — just with better coffee.
